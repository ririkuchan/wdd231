Site Plan

Project: Tokyo Tea Trail

1. Site Name

Tokyo Tea Trail — A curated guide to artisan tea shops and modern tea culture across Tokyo.

2. Site Purpose

This site helps visitors discover outstanding tea spots in Tokyo and learn simple brewing methods. The content includes (1) shop guides with maps, (2) tasting notes for Japanese and Chinese teas, and (3) downloadable checklists for home brewing essentials.

3. Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. “Where can I find a quiet tea shop near Shibuya for a first Sencha tasting?”
  2. “What minimal tools do I need to brew Gyokuro at home?”
  3. “Which shops host weekend tea events or workshops?”
